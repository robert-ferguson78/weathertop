Welcome to WeatherTop

The WeatherTop website is a global platform where anyone can automatically upload data from their own automatic weather station to there registered Weather stations.

There will be an official launch later this year but you can already access the site at http://localhost:9000/ If you own a weather station you are very welcome to connect it to the site. We’d love to hear how you get on and would value any feedback!

WeatherTop is a great tool which allows weather observations to to be displayed in real-time. The observations are from different sources, the quality varies from source to source and the data are not reviewed or corrected. Data may be missing from time to time and occasional spikes may occur.

WeatherTop has great potential for use as an outreach and education platform. It is not the official website for weather data from National weather stations. Quality controlled data from Met Éireann’s official weather stations are available at https://www.met.ie/climate/available-data.
